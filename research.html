Working with us

What kinds of problems do you take?

Problems that require original thinking — mechanisms not yet understood, results that need explaining, questions that don't have a known answer yet. We work across biology, materials, food science, and computation.

Do you work with academic labs?

Yes. We regularly collaborate with university groups — on translation problems, mechanism investigation, and work that needs to move from lab to application.

Can you respond to RFPs and open challenges?

Yes — we track hard-problem calls and open challenges and respond as a team that can execute the science, not just write the proposal.
