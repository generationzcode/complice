Working with us

What does a Build engagement look like?

Usually a problem where the science is clear but the system doesn't exist yet — or exists but doesn't scale. We come in at the design layer, building the process or platform from the science up.

Do you work with biotech startups?

Yes. Particularly early-stage companies that have a result or a proof of concept and need to turn it into something that runs reliably at larger scale.

What about digital and platform work?

We build platforms where the underlying content or curriculum is scientific — education infrastructure, data tools, lab-facing dashboards. Always science-led, not technology-led.
