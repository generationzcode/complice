Working with us

What do you look for in a potential filing?

Novelty, non-obviousness, and a clear description of what the invention actually is. We often find claim pathways that prior assessments missed — particularly in natural-compound and biological process work.

Do you work with academic groups and startups?

Yes — both. Academic labs often have protectable work they haven't filed. Startups often have weak or incomplete filings. We work with both situations.

What's the natural-versus-synthetic thesis?

A recurring theme in our portfolio — that natural-origin processes, compounds, and organisms can be as effective as synthetic alternatives, and are increasingly protectable on novel process and application claims.
