Science for
the next
generation.

We design and run science education programmes — for schools, NGOs, and institutions that want children to encounter science the way scientists do: with real questions, real materials, and real results.

Schools Teachers NGOs Institutions After-school

Programme areas

01
Biology
in the field
Soil, water, plants, and microbes — observed directly, not from a diagram. Programmes built around environments children already live in.
02
Food
science
Fermentation, nutrition, and what happens to food over time. Experiments children can run in a classroom or kitchen without specialised equipment.
03
Science
as inquiry
Methods, questions, hypotheses, and surprise. A programme designed around the practice of science, not just its conclusions. For children aged 8–16.

Working with
schools and NGOs.

We partner with schools, after-school programmes, and science access organisations. We design the materials, train the facilitators, and run the programmes ourselves where needed.
