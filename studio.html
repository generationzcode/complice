Working with us

Who do you produce scicomm for?

Science-based companies and research institutions — agritech, food tech, biotech, materials, and deeptech companies who need their science communicated accurately. Also academic groups preparing grant materials, reports, and public-facing content.

What types of outputs do you produce?

Illustrated mechanisms, product science briefs, academic figures, education content, slide decks, annual reports, researcher profiles — and websites for deeptech and science-first companies where the technical content is the core of the site.

How technical do you go?

All the way. We write and illustrate from primary sources and our own understanding of the science — not from summaries. We flag it when we're uncertain about something rather than smoothing over it.

Who are the deeptech websites for?

Specifically for companies where the science is the product — spinouts, early-stage deeptech, and research-led companies where generic web agency output would misrepresent the work. Not for general commercial sites.
